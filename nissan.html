Nissan Internship

Because of the secretive nature of this project, I signed a non-disclosure agreement. As such, all images and information on this page is taken from press releases and articles approved by Nissan.

I spent the summers of 2014 and 2015 at the Nissan Research Center in Sunnyvale, California. I worked on a team of software developers and roboticists working on creating an autonomous vehicle prototyping platform.

My work focused on the installation and integration of sensors as some work on the fusing of data we received from them. Unfortunately, I cannot go into detail about what I was working on. I learned a lot over my two summers there, especially about software development as a team. While at Nissan, I learned how to use Git, Agile Development and Scrum. I also learned a lot about the importance of readable code and documentation.
